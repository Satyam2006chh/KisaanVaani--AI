### **Bimari ki Pehchan**
**Bimari ka Naam:** Tamatar ka **Ageti Jhulsa (Early Blight)**.  
**Karak (Pathogen):** Yeh *Alternaria solani* naamak phaphoond (fungus) ke karan hota hai.  
**Confidence Level:** **95% sambhavna** hai ki yeh Early Blight hi hai.

### **Lakshan (Symptoms):**
1. Aap patti par dekh sakte hain ki dhabbe gol hain aur unke andar dhyan se dekhne par **'Target Board'** jaise gol chhalle (concentric rings) dikhayi de rahe hain.
2. In dhabbon ke chaaro taraf ek peela ghera (yellow halo) hai, jo patti ko kamzor kar raha hai.
3. Agar ise roka nahi gaya, to yeh dheere-dheere poore paudhe aur tamatar ke phalon par bhi fail sakta hai.

### **Samadhan aur Upchar (Remedies):**

**1. Rasayanik Upchar (Chemical Control):**
*   **Mancozeb 75% WP:** 2 se 2.5 gram prati litre pani mein gholkar chidkaav karein.
*   **Chlorothalonil 75% WP:** 2 gram prati litre pani mein milakar spray karein.
*   Agar bimari zyada fail chuki hai, to **Azoxystrobin 18.2% + Difenoconazole 11.4% SC** (jaise Amistar Top) ka 1 ml prati litre pani ke hisab se upyog karein.

**2. Jaivik Upchar (Organic Remedy):**
*   **Neem Oil:** 5 ml Neem ka tel aur thoda sa sabun ka pani milakar 1 litre pani mein milayein aur chidkaav karein.
*   **Trichoderma viride:** 5 gram prati litre pani mein milakar mitti aur paudhon par spray karein.

**3. Krishi Prabandhan (Management Tips):**
*   Paudhe ke neeche ki wo pattiyaan jo mitti se chhoo rahi hain, unhe turant kaat kar khet se door phenk dein ya jala dein.
*   Paudhon ke beech hava ka sanchar (air circulation) achha rakhein.
*   Sinchayi hamesha jadon ke paas karein, pattiyon par pani girne se bachein kyunki nami se fungus tezi se failta hai.
