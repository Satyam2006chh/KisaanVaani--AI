### **AGRI-SCIENTIST REPORT: TAMATAR FASAL VISHLESHAN**

#### **1. BIMARI KI PEHCHAN:**
Is bimari ka naam **'Agheti Jhulsa' (Early Blight)** hai. Iska vaigyanik naam ***Alternaria solani*** hai. Ye ek phafund (fungus) janit rog hai jo tamatar ki fasal ko bhari nuksan pahunchata hai.

#### **2. LAKSHAN (Symptoms):**
Photo mein dikh rahe teen pramukh lakshan ye hain:
*   **Target Spots (Bull’s Eye):** Patti ke beech mein bhoore rang ka dhabba hai jisme gol-gol challe (concentric rings) saaf dikhayi de rahe hain. Ye is bimari ki sabse badi pehchan hai.
*   **Chlorotic Halo:** Bhoore dhabbe ke charon taraf ek peele rang ka ghera (yellow halo) ban gaya hai, jo darshata hai ki bimari tezi se patti ke swasth hisse ko maar rahi hai.
*   **Tissue Necrosis:** Dhabbe ka kendriya hissa sookh kar kaala/bhoora ho gaya hai, jisse patti ki prakash sanshleshan (photosynthesis) karne ki kshamata khatam ho rahi hai.

#### **3. CONFIDENCE LEVEL:**
Main is pehchan par **98% sure** hoon.

#### **4. TURANT ILAJ (Chemical):**
Fasal ko bachane ke liye aap inme se kisi ek ka chhidkaw turant karein:
*   **Mancozeb 75% WP:** 2.5 gram dawa ko 1 liter paani mein milakar chhidkaw karein.
*   **Azoxystrobin 18.2% + Difenoconazole 11.4% SC:** 1 ml dawa ko 1 liter paani mein milakar spray karein (Ye bahut prabhavshali hai).

#### **5. JAIVIK ILAJ (Organic):**
Agar aap jaivik kheti kar rahe hain:
*   **Neem Oil:** 3000 PPM wala neem tel 5 ml prati liter paani mein milakar spray karein.
*   **Trichoderma viride:** 5 gram prati liter paani mein milakar pattiyon par chhidkaw karein aur jadon ke paas bhi dein.

#### **6. SAVDHANI (Prevention):**
*   **Neeche ki pattiyan hatayein:** Paudhe ke nichle hisse ki jo pattiyan zameen ko chhu rahi hain, unhe turant kaat kar khet se door phenk dein ya jala dein.
*   **Sinchai ka dhyan:** Paani hamesha jadon mein dein, pattiyon par paani girne se phafund tezi se phailti hai.
*   **Fasal Chakra:** Agli baar tamatar wale khet mein baigan ya aloo na lagayein.
